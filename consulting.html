Engineering Consulting

Practical engineering support for difficult manufacturing problems.

Independent consulting focused on plastics processing, product design, manufacturing problem solving, and technical training. Work is selective and generally centered on complex or high-risk issues where standard approaches have failed or are insufficient.

Engagement

Consulting and training engagements are typically arranged through existing professional relationships, referral, or direct inquiry.

Areas of Focus

  • Injection molding process optimization
  • Scientific Injection Molding (S.I.M.) methods
  • Part design and failure analysis
  • Tooling, materials, and manufacturing process review
  • Production problem resolution and root cause analysis
  • Design-for-manufacturing and practical launch support

Training & Seminars

Training is available for engineering teams, technicians, and manufacturing personnel who need practical, real-world instruction rather than theory alone.

  • Scientific Injection Molding (S.I.M.)
  • Root cause analysis in production environments
  • Failure mode identification and correction
  • Design-for-manufacturing principles
  • Process discipline for difficult or unstable production conditions

Approach

The focus is straightforward: understand the problem, identify what is actually driving it, and correct what matters. The goal is not theory for its own sake. It is usable improvement in design, process, or manufacturing performance.

This site serves as both an author page and a professional reference point for engineering consulting and training.
