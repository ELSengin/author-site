Books

Your Word Is Your Worth

Memoir / Narrative Nonfiction

A memoir tracing a life shaped by farm work, military policing, structural ironwork, and engineering. The book centers on responsibility, decision-making under uncertainty, and the way a personal code is formed across very different worlds of work.

My Favorite Pair of Boots

Poetry collection

A poetry manuscript built from the same life arc, exploring labor, duty, risk, endurance, memory, and the marks left by a life of work and consequence.
